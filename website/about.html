About Us


The University of Jisc is a leading public research university located in the coastal city of Jisc, England.


Founded in 1903, the university has a long and distinguished history of academic excellence. Today, the university offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across a variety of disciplines, including arts, humanities, social sciences, sciences, engineering, and medicine.


The University of Jisc is home to over 15,000 students from over 100 countries. The university is committed to creating a diverse and inclusive learning environment, and it offers a wide range of support services to help students from all backgrounds succeed.

Location


The university is located in a beautiful setting on the edge of the English Channel. The campus is home to a number of historic buildings, including the Old Quad, which dates back to the 19th century. The university also has a number of modern facilities, including a state-of-the-art library, a sports complex, and a number of student union buildings.


The University of Jisc is a member of the Russell Group, a prestigious association of research-intensive universities in the UK. The university is also a member of the European University Association, and it has partnerships with universities around the world.
